hello, world Because I have the map itself (i.e. I've identified the potential greyfield parcels and they are in separate layers) and I'm working on the Map Viewer later, here's a guide to the layer names. In the map viewer, you can click them to hide or show different layers: - parcels_ONLYsewer = Non-residential parcels with sewer available, but not connected; they have no data for water or gas connection. They have an impervious surface cover of greater than 15% - parcels_ONLYwatersewer = Non-residential parcels with water and sewer available, but not connected; they have no data for gas connection. They have an impervious surface cover of greater than 15% - parcels_ONLYwater = Non-residential parcels with water available, but not connected; they have no data for sewer or gas connection. They have an impervious surface cover of greater than 15% - parcels_ALLavailable = Non-residential parcels with water, sewer, and gas available, but not connected. They have impervious surface area of greater than 15% - parcels_impervious = Non-residential parcels with an impervious surface cover of greater than 15% - parcels = Non-residential parcels
